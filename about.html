About AbacCondo

The easiest way for students to find housing near their university

🏠 Our Mission

AbacCondo was built by students, for students. We know how stressful it is to find affordable, safe, and convenient housing near campus — especially when you're far from home.

Our mission is simple: make it ridiculously easy to find your perfect condo near ABAC (Assumption University) and beyond. No middleman fees, no sketchy listings — just real condos posted by real people.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is AbacCondo free to use?
Yes! AbacCondo is 100% free for both tenants and landlords. No listing fees, no commission.
How do I post a listing?
Sign up or log in, then click the "Post Listing" button. Fill in the details and you're live in seconds.
Can I delete my listing?
Absolutely. Log in and you'll see a delete button on all your listings. You can also manage them from your profile page.
Which areas do you cover?
We currently focus on the ABAC (Assumption University) Bang Na area, including Bearing, Samrong, and nearby neighborhoods. More universities coming soon!
How do I contact a landlord?
Click on a listing to see the details. Contact info and directions are provided. You can also reach us directly and we'll connect you.
